Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide safe, family-friendly fun for all who come to our wonderful establishment! We ensure that all our guests, young and old, will feel content and comfortable as the wild rapids propel them along the river.

Audience

Who is my target audience? My target audience predominantly consists of families and other groups of people who are looking for an affordable, exciting, and fun experience on the water. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? My audience will primarily access my site on desktop/laptop, however, the page will be designed so that it is accessible on any device. I believe it will be most popular on desktop due to its focus on information; text-heavy websites are ideally viewed on a larger screen. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
